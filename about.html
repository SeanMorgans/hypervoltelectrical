Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2019, Hypervolt Electrical began with a simple mission: to provide South Wales with electrical services that prioritize safety, quality, and customer satisfaction above all else.

A small family business countless satisfied customers across Cardiff, Newport, Swansea, and the surrounding areas.

Our commitment to excellence has earned us full insurance coverage, and most importantly, the trust of our community. We believe in doing the job right the first time, using only the highest quality materials and adhering to the latest safety standards.

Today, we continue to grow while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that built our reputation. Every project, from a simple socket installation to a complete commercial rewiring, receives the same level of professional care and expertise.
